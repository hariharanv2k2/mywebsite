Student Management System

About the System

The Student Management System (SMS) is designed to streamline and manage student information, including personal details, academic records, and course enrollment. It aims to enhance the efficiency of administrative tasks and improve the overall educational experience.

Features

  • Student Registration
  • Course Enrollment
  • Grade Management
  • Attendance Tracking
  • Reporting and Analytics
  • Admin Dashboard

How It Works

The system allows administrators to manage student data, enroll students in courses, track attendance, and record grades. Students can access their own profiles to view their academic progress and update personal information. The system provides various reports to help educators and administrators make informed decisions.
